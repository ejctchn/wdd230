passing through this town on a family vacation. - Cooped up in a car for many hours. Wants to get out and have fun
with the family. "If one of my kids screams one more time, I'm going to go crazy. I need to find something for us all to do."

pool - Needs to locate businesses in the area to promote their analitical services to. "If I don't do well with this my
